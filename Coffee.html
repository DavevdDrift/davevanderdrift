  • The baristagair app

    Date: 26/11/2018

  • About
    For a groupsproject I developed a learning tool app for the company N-Ice.Coffee. The company teaches people who find it hard to learn things. They train these people to become baristas. These people find it hard to learn and practice assignments. That is why we were asked if we could make something helpfull. For this project we delivered several works, which are divided into a research report and a design report.

    Research question
    The current curriculum is good, however, the program is not supplemented enough outside the teaching hours. This ensures that they won’t learn as good. It also ensures hat they aren’t stimulated enough to learn. As a result, they don’t develop confidence fast enough and they have less pleasure in learning.

    Result
    Through research, design and testing, an application has been developed that serves as a learning tool. During the exhibition we received a standing applause from our client.

    The curriculum offered knowledge in the shape of practice video’s and practice, which are achieved through courses, among other things, which allow people to practice their knowledge.

    Secondly, students must be and remain stimulated, this was achieved by using gamification. Using a scoreboard, students can achieve their own score and battle for the highest one against each other.

    Thirdly, self-confidence had to be increased. That was made possible by rewarding the student and showing their progress.

    Finally, baristagairs should also enjoy it. This was achieved by using things such as gamification and Qoutes.
