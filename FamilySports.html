  • Remake Family Sports app

    Date: 24/11/2020
    Grade: 8.0

  • About
    Family Sports has an app. The app explains exercises and has different functions, such as testing your BMI. In addition, the app also provides information about the company itself, such as news, contact information, schedules for (group)lessons and information about their company. However, the app had major room for improvement.

    Research Question
    “What does the Family Sports app need to make using the app more exciting in order to get assistance and knowledge about exercises, diet, information and news about the company. ”

    Conclusion
    The hi-fi prototype of the app represents the research and answers the main question. An application has been developed that guides the user with training, nutrition and progression tracking. The user can look up various types of information about nutrition, training and Family Sports itself.

    Result
    New functions have been added, old functions have been improved, the User Experience has been improved and the visual design got an update. Because of this, the app will be better at informing the User about Family Sports, guide the user with their training and diet and it makes the user more excited to exercise.
