  • Digitization Klandizie Game

    Date: 24/06/2021
    Grade: 8.0

  • About Klandizie
    For my graduation project I digitized the Klandizie board game. The physical version of the Klandizie board game couldn’t be played, due to Corona and the few amount of physical copies. The game did not have a digital version, which could offer the same effectiveness and playful experience as the physical version.

    The goal was to digitize the board game, so that the target audience can easily and safely play the game during the Corona times. In doing so, i had to investigate which way of digitization gives the most effective product. The final product must stimulate a positive customer- and gaming experience. In addition, it was necessary to find the most efficient way to access and download the game.

    The process
    I started with research, using a product vision board, deskresearch, target group research and a concurrent analysis. The research showed that a mobile application would be the best way to digitize the board game. It also showed that having the same playstyle and experience is important.

    The application could work in multiple ways, with different features. I made multiple concept scenario’s, that showed how certain features could work. Preference was given to the idea of the multiplayer mode, where an assessor judges the open answers.

    I started making designs based on the chosen concept scenario’s. I started by making a sitemap, followed by wireframes. After some feedback and changes I started making a styleguide, hi-fi designs, followed by a prototype. This was done with the help of the program Figma.

    At the end I did multiple tests with the target group. The participants got to play the prototype, while I asked them some question. With the help of their feedback I made final changes the the design and prototype.

    The result
    The final result is a hi-fi prototype of the Klandizie board game, in the form of a mobile application. You can play the game, look up the tutorial & news about the game, add new questions to keep the gameplay fresh, download new questions so you can use them in the physical version of the game, give feedback to improve the app. The game itsself also has a leaderboard, to add more competition and a chat function, in order to communicate well.
