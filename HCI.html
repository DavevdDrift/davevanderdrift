  • Making a Virtual Reality game

    Date: 10/07/2019
    Grade: 7.5

  • About
    In the course "Human Computer Interaction" I got to know a new side of my education. I got in touch with new kinds of special elektronics, programms and equipment. A lot of cool and unique programms and machines can be made with these things.

    I have worked with many subjects during the Human Computer Interaction course. These topics are Wearables, Playfull Interactions, Artificial Creatures and Augmented / Virtual Reality.

    The result
    Together with my project group I worked on creating a virtual game. The object of this game is to overcome phobias by playing the game. Virtual Reality provides a realistic immersion, which helps creating a realistic but same simulation. This caused some fear for many people, but also a lot of fun.

    The idea was to make people do something they would never dare doing in real life. That’s how this game tries to help people overcome their fears, in order to improve their lives.
