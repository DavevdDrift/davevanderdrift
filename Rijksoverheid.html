  • A package of appreciation

    Date: 25/01/2021
    Grade: 8.5

  • About
    Together with a project group I worked on making a service for people who are caregivers of their demented parent. This service ensures that the caregiver receives appreciation from the initial phase, is prepared for the process and can relax in this difficult time.

    Issue
    The government is compartmentalized: there are hundreds of organizations with their own tasks and responsibilities; the caregiver is sent from pillar to post.

    Objective
    Gaining insight into the bottlenecks of informal carers in order to design an optimal user experience that can offer the right service from the national government.

    Result
    To show how the service could look like, a poster has been made.The poster shows a possible look of the package, along with the contents of the package. The package includes a “thank you” card from the Minister of Health, a diary to write down beautiful moments, a disposable camera to record those moments, a checklist to see what things need to be arranged, a booklet with tips on how to relax and a bracelet to show someone with pride that he or she is a pride caregiver. In addition, a storyboard has been created to show how the service works. The end result came about through extensive research.
